About


    Founded in 1919, Nankai University's postgraduate education can be traced back to 1928. Since the founding of the National Southwest Associated University and New China and the reform and opening up till now, Nankai University has been engaging in postgraduate education and training for more than 80 years. As a multidisciplinary and research-based university with equal emphasis on Liberal Arts and Sciences and a complete range of disciplines, Nankai University has formed a unique postgraduate education and training model.


    In September 1984, with the approval of the former State Education Commission, Nankai University was among the first 22 universities in the country to pilot a graduate school. Since then, the graduate education of Nankai University has seen unprecedented development in terms of both the level and the scale of education and school management. It has embarked on a path of standardized and professional development.


    In the 30 years since the establishment of our Graduate School, Nankai University has always regarded discipline construction as its top priority, which in turn, gives a strong impetus to the development in other areas. Currently, there are 26 colleges (departments, institutes) and research units in Nankai University with postgraduate enrollment qualifications, covering 13 disciplines, namely Philosophy, Economics, Law, History, Literature, Education, Mechanics, Engineering, Medicine, Agriculture, Management Science, Military Science and Art. As an important institution responsible for the discipline construction planning of the whole university and the education of more than 10,000 postgraduates, the Graduate School of Nankai University has become a key one with great influence in China.


    We are dedicated to recruiting talents based on their merits. Nankai University will always open its door to capable, productive, and professional talents, and provide well-equipped facilities as best as it can to serve its scientific research and teaching. There have been outstanding talents in older generations such as Chen Shengshen, Wu Dayou and Cao Yu; Now there are a group of young and middle-aged talents led by the elder generation. At present, Nankai University has formed a well-organized teaching team with academic excellence and dedication to innovation.


    Over the past 80 years, the postgraduate education of Nankai University has adhered to the development concept of ruling the country by literature, strengthening the country with science and enriching the country with commerce, the concept advocated by our founding President Zhang Boling and closely related to social practices. In addition, through upholding the principle of making study to serve our country, the postgraduate education in Nankai University serves China in society, politics and economy, and has formed a down-to-earth academic style in doing research.


    As an ancient Chinese proverb goes, good honing gives a sharp edge to a sword, and bitter cold adds more fragrance to plum blossom. After years of strenuous efforts, we have striven to get our school to enter the ranks of world-class universities, serving as the representative and leader of advanced educational concepts, practices, and systems. Meanwhile, we also aim to cultivate talents with innovation and great ambitions in the process of postgraduate education and cultivation. All of these will contribute to improving our education quality, academic excellence and overall school-management level, and attain advanced world level in some of our leading disciplines, making Nankai University an important source of high-level professionals with creativity and knowledge innovation in China.
