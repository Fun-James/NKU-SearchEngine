  • Engaged in the research of Japanese thought and culture, mainly focusing on the comparison of Chinese and Japanese Confucianism. Her research on Japanese spiritual and cultural characteristics is in a leading position in China. Presides over one major project and one general project of social science at provincial and ministerial level, and undertakes one sub-project of major project of the Ministry of Education. Vice president of the Chinese Society of Japanese Philosophy and executive director of the Chinese Society of Japanese History.
