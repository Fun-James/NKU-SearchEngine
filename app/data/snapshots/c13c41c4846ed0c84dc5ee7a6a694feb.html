  • Vice Dean of CFL Wang Kai

Wang Kai, Vice Dean of CFL.

Male, born in 1981. Postgraduate doctoral degree, associate professor and master supervisor of Nankai University. Vice dean of the College of Foreign Languages.

Research interest: Ancient Japanese literature, ancient Japanese history and the history of the formation of Post-war Japanese Metropolitan Area.

Responsibilities: Responsible for undergraduate teaching, international exchanges and cooperation, foreign teacher management and service, international talent training and information technology service, etc.
