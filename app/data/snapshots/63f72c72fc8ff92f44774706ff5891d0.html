Responsibilities of the Office for Humanities & Social Sciences

The organization and management of humanities & social sciences research activities

(1) Planning and implementation for Humanities & social science research development

(2) "2011 Plan" collaborative innovation related work

(3) Process management for all categories research projects and organization of achievement awards

(4) Collaborative management the research funding

(5) The organization to declare various outstanding achievement awards

(6) Construction of humanities & social science research base managed by the Ministry of Education and Tianjin

(7) Management for non-entity research institutions of humanities and social sciences

(8) Promote domestic and international cooperation in the field of humanities and social sciences

(9) Archives management and information system construction for humanities and social science research

(10) Other work assigned by higher authorities
