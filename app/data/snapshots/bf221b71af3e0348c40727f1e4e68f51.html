    Education

    From 1999 to 2002, she studied for a master's degree, majoring in English Language and Literature at Nankai University. she joined the School of Foreign Languages of Nankai University in 2002 and has been teaching since.

    From 2013 to 2017, she studied at the School of Foreign Languages of Nankai University with a PhD in Foreign Literature.

    From August 2015 to February 2016, she studied at the University of Hawaii in the United States.

    At present, she is mainly engaged in the research of American literature and contemporary western literary theory.
