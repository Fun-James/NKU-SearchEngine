Beginning of Medical Educationat Nankai University
The founder of Nankai University, Mr. Zhang Boling first planned to initiate medical education in this University before he started the premed program and the preparatory group of medical school. In 1952, the first class of students from Tianjin Medical College (now known as Tianjin Medical University) attended the premed program at Nankai University.
