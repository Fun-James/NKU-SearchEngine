  • Nankai Advisory Club

    Nankai Advisory Club (NAC) is a group of Nankai students and alumni who are passionate about consulting. As a platform for connection, NAC is committed to delivering consulting insights as well as providing networking and job searching resources for Nankai students who are interested in careers in the consulting field. The goal of NAC is to promote Nankai students to make a positive impact on the consulting field in China.
