  • Lecturer of the Italian Department of the college of foreign languages Nankai University. She has completed her PhD from the University of Milan (2023), MA from the University of Padova (2019), and BA in Italian Studies from Sichuan International Studies University (China) (2017). Her research interests focus on cultural and linguistic exchanges between Italy and China during the period of Renaissance. She is the research member of project OIM (Italianismi nel mondo) founded by Accademia della Crusca and the academic assistant at Accademia Ambrosiana. She holds a CILS C2 certificate for the Italian proficiency exam.
