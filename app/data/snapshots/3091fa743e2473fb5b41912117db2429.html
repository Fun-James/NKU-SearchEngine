The School of Finance is being established as a part of Nankai University to meet the needs of the changing international markets. The school was set up as part of the redevelopment of Nankai University's discipline of Economics. A new campus is being constructed in Jinnan to the highest of international standards for academic institutions, and will provide a conducive environment for academic pursuit and achievement.
