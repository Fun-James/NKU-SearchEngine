Youth League Committee


The Communist Youth League (Student Affairs Office) is responsible for student affairs. Our responsibilities are:


1. To assist directors of the college to give students proper guidance and to help with the directors’ administration;

2. To assist the college Party committee to do the party construction work;

3. To be in charge of the work of the Communist Youth League;

4. To be in charge of student orientation;

5. To be in charge of psychological counseling;

6. To be in charge of supervising students’ personal development;

7. To be in charge of the Student Scholarships administration and students’ involvement in community service;

8. To give guidance to student union and other student organizations;

9. To help with students’ hunting for jobs;

10. To be in charge of receiving and managing new students’ records;

11. To select students for overseas exchange programs;

12. To assist in organizing students’ internship;

13. To accomplish other work assigned by senior officers.
