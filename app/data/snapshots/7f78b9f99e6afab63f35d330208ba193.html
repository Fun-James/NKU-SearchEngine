  • Foreign Language Exam Center of Nankai University

The Foreign Language Examination Center of Nankai University was established in 2004 with the approval of the Examination Center of the Ministry of Education. The office is located in the College of Foreign Languages and is responsible for the organization and management of examinations in various languages. Currently, the test items that have been opened are: IELTS, TOEFL , GRE , GMAT, TestDaF, JLPT, TOPIK, BJT, TOEIC, PETS, BULATS, FECT, etc.
